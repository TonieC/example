About

A quick hello,
not a press release

I'm Kia — I organize events, advocate for causes I genuinely believe in, and occasionally speak about them in rooms full of people. Based in Quezon City, I work at the crossroads of community, business, and humanitarian action.

My work spans organizing and producing events, leading or participating in social impact initiatives, and building connections between people and organizations that have something meaningful to offer each other.

I write too — about the work, about what I've learned, and about the quiet things that keep me going. Not trying to be a thought leader. Just someone who shows up and does the work.
