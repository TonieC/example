About me

A hello, not a
press release

I'm Kia — a student from Quezon City who organizes events, speaks about things I believe in, and is slowly, consciously figuring out who I want to become. This site is less of a portfolio and more of a living document of that journey.

"Less 'look at my achievements' and more 'here's what I'm learning, who I'm meeting, and what's keeping me up at night (in a good way).'"

My work lives at the crossroads of community organizing, humanitarian action, and the business of making good things happen. I've led events, joined organizations, spoken on panels, and done a lot of work that doesn't fit neatly on a résumé.

I write too — about the work, about the uncertainty, about matcha and flowers and the small beautiful things. Not trying to be a thought leader. Just showing up, being honest, and inviting you along.
