About

A tiny intro,
not a TED Talk

I'm Kia — a creative dev who likes clean layouts, playful color palettes, and interfaces that feel friendly instead of corporate. I'm based in Quezon City and I spend most of my time making small things for the web.

I enjoy writing about what I build, what I learn, and the small things that keep me curious. If there's a weird CSS bug involved, I'll probably write a whole post about it.

Not trying to hustle culture my way through life — just making things I'm actually proud of, one small project at a time.
